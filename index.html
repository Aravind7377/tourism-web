Welcome to Courtallam

The Land of Majestic Waterfalls

Cortallam Tourism Courtallam, also known as Kutralam, is a perfect getaway from Tenkasi and
Tirunelveli.A small town bordering Kollam District, Courtallam is famous for its waterfalls on the Western
Ghats. With captivating panoramic vistas, the township is popularly renowned as 'Spa of South'.
It has nine waterfalls in the region that add a charm to its exotic beauty. One will also find Kutrallam adorned with
ancient temples reflecting the age-old legends attached to them. The town itself
like many other cities of India narrates mythological folklores adding to its mystique.

PLACES

One of the prime attractions of all the nine waterfalls, Main Falls also known as Peraruvi is situated in the town center. It falls from an altitude of 60m and serves as the biggest of all the falls in the area. In fact, the water stream besides being a major sightseeing spot, is also place of worship for locals.
The water here splits into five branches while falling, so it is called Five Falls. It is unique as water flows in five directions, resembling a 5-headed cobra. It is highly revered among locals as they compare it to Adishesha. Among the five falls, three are open to men and two for women.
